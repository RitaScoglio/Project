Due to its retitling when it was released to television in the 50s, the film has become better known by its reissue name One for the Book. 

==Plot==
A young actress agrees to a date with a soldier on a weekend pass after hes stood up by her friend. When the soldier has trouble getting a hotel room he ends up spending the weekend at her apartment.
