Puli Bebbuli
 
 Tollywood film directed by K. S. R. Das. This film stars Krishnam Raju, Chiranjeevi, Jaya Prada and Raadhika in the lead roles.

==Cast==
*Krishnam Raju as Raj Kumar
*Chiranjeevi as Gopi Krishna
*Jaya Prada as Sita
*Raadhika as Sitas younger sister
*Kanta Rao as Gajapati
*Mikkilineni Radhakrishna Murthy as Rajs adopted father
*Kaikala Satyanarayana
*Tiger Prabhakar
*Kakarala as Sitas father
