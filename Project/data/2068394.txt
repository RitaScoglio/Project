The Sterling Chase
The Sterling Chase, also known as Graduation Week, is a drama film starring Nicholle Tom, Jack Noseworthy, Devon Odessa, Sean Patrick Thomas, Alanna Ubach and John Livingston. The independent film was written and directed by Tanya Fenmore, and was screened at the Mill Valley Film Festival on September 17, 1999 (see 1999 in film).  Screen story was written by Jeremy Dauber, currently an assistant professor at Columbia University.
