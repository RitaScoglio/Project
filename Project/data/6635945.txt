==The story==

The year is 1964, and Carolina Military Institute is about to do something it has never done in its entire history—allow a black man to become a member of the Corps of Cadets. Will McLean, a senior private, has been assigned to watch over him to ensure that he makes it through the year.  This assignment forces him to stand against the power of "the Ten", an alleged secret brotherhood within the Corps of Cadets sworn to uphold CMIs highest standards—by any and all means necessary.
