 drama crime Kevin Lewis, starring Huntley Ritter, Krista Allen, Charles S. Dutton and Chloë Grace Moretz.  It is released on February 8, 2008. 

== Plot ==
A young man is sentenced to life in prison for killing two children, a crime he didnt commit. DNA evidence sets him free, but there is no hiding from the prison gang that wants him dead. When his own daughter is abducted and apparently murdered, he seeks revenge. How far will he go and is it ever too late for redemption?
