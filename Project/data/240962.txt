Amy Stillers Breast is a 2000 short comedy film starring Amy Stiller as herself, and featuring her parents Jerry Stiller and Anne Meara as themselves. It was directed by Becky Neiman and written by Henry Joseph, Becky Neiman, and Amy Stiller. Produced by Henry Joseph.

==Plot== Madonna has appeared on her left breast. 

==Awards==
In 2000, the Grand Jury Prize for Best Short Film was awarded to Becky Neiman for her work.
