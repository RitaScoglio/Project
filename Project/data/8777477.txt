Saving Angelo
Saving 2007  boxer that he and his family rescued in 2003. All promotions and earnings wents to animal charity projects.

In January 2007, Kay filed a lawsuit claiming that producer Conroy Kanter "unfairly demanded creative control and distribution rights" for the film. {{cite news | title = Producer Responds to `Angelo Lawsuit | publisher = Associated Press | date = 2007-02-07 | accessdate = 2007-05-12 
| url = http://abcnews.go.com/Entertainment/wireStory?id=2856324&CMP=OTC-RSSFeeds0312 }}    The suit was settled out of court with  Kay receiving "full ownership and control" of the film, along with a $20,000 donation to Crusades for Animals.
