Heavens Fire is a 1999 made-for-TV action movie film starring Eric Roberts and Jürgen Prochnow.

==Plot==
The story involves a group of would-be burglars who attempt a daring daylight robbery of a Federal Building in Seattle, Washington|Seattle, Washington (U.S. state)|Washington.  However, the criminals are forced to improvise when their getaway helicopter crashes and sets a deadly fire in the building.  Also in the building is a former security guard (Eric Roberts) who is the only person who can stop the heist and save the innocent bystanders who are trapped in the burning building.
