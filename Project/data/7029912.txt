 1948 film Slovene sound feature film. It was released on 21 November 1948 in Union Cinema ( ) in Ljubljana, received a great public acclaim, and was entered into the 1949 Cannes Film Festival.   

== Plot ==
  German army Partisan resistance forces and the villagers. 

==Filming==
The film was shot on location in the black-and-white technique in the villages of Grahovo ob Bači and Koritnica, Tolmin|Koritnica. 

==Music==
The music for the film was written by the composer Marjan Kozina.
