Let Me Die a Woman (1978 in film|1978) is a semi-documentary film by exploitation film director Doris Wishman. 

==Plot== sex reassignment surgeon Dr. Leo Wollman and various transgender | trans people.

Between the interviews, there are re-enactments or dramatizations of the interviewees experiences, including an interview with Deborah Hartin. One interview features a woman who didnt wait long enough after surgery before she took a man home, and her new vagina started to bleed because it hadnt been given enough time to heal.
