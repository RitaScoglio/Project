The film based on the Ancient Greek god Aphrodite is an independent film production filmed in Mali. All of the actors are from Mali and only ever appeared in this film. The film was produced by an Argentine production team.

==Cast==
* Issa Coulibaly Alejandro Da Silva

* Sacko Kante
* Fatoumata Coulibaly
* Fatoumata Yerle
* Rokia Daiwara
* Yiriba Coulibaly

==Plot== Zephyrs find a young adolescent naked and sleeping in the borders of the sea. With no doubt, they determine that it is Aphrodite. They take the boy to Citrea where his status is judged. There he becomes a conflicting personage of ambiguous sexuality.

==Release==
The film premiered on 15 October 1998 in Buenos Aires. It was an independent film production and most of the actors only ever appeared in this film.
