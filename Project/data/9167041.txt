Agnipankh (The wings of fire in Hindi) is an Indian film directed by Sanjiv Puri about pilots in the Indian Air Force. It was released in 2004.

== Synopsis ==
 Rahul Dev Anjana is Surbhi is a wealthy socialite who loathes flying but falls for Siddharth. However, Sameer falls for Surbhi while being unaware of Anjanas love for him.
 Pakistani Rangers chasing them for the kill.
