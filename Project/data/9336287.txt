The Kiss Before the Mirror (1933 in film|1933) is a suspense film directed by James Whale, starring Nancy Carroll, Frank Morgan, Gloria Stuart, and Walter Pidgeon, and released by Universal Pictures.

==Plot==
A lawyer (Morgan) realizes that his own wife (Carroll) might be having an affair while he defends a cuckolded wife-killer.

==Remake== remade by Whale five years later as Wives Under Suspicion.
