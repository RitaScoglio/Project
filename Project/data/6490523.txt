==Plot summary==
 
When a team of undercover narcotics officers is targeted by a serial killer, the police recruit karate champion Matt Logan (Chuck Norris) to bring the murders to an end. Narcotics Officer Amanda "Mandy" Rust (Jennifer ONeill) discovers that a traitor within the police ranks is behind the killings.

==Reception==
 
Critics nicknamed the film "A Farce of One" though it did manage to gross over twenty million dollars domestically.
