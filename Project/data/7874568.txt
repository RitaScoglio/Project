Desserts is a 1998 short film directed by Jeff Stark and starring Ewan McGregor.

==Cast==
*Ewan McGregor as Stroller

==Plot==

A man (the stroller) is walking along a beach and finds a chocolate Éclair (pastry)|éclair, ponders over it for a bit then decides to eat it, then suddenly is yanked into the sea by a hook and line.
