Better Living Through Circuitry is a 1999   founder Brian Perera.   

==Participants==

The documentary features in-depth interviews with Moby, Wolfgang Flür, Brian Transeau, DJ Spooky, DJ Keoki, The Crystal Method, Roni Size, Electric Skychurch, Carl Cox, Frankie Bones.
 Club Shelter, are part of the permanent collection  of the Smithsonian Museum and the Pure Children/Earth Program Collective   who worked and lived in a New York City loft space from which they created graphics, promoted raves and managed their own record label.  

== Production ==

=== Filming ===
Filming started in March 1997, with a budget of $100,000, at the Winter Music Conference.  VX1000 digital raves in a backpack.

==Post Production==
Virtually all post-production including editing, on-line, titles and effects were all performed in a spare bedroom of the producers apartment using the Media 100 non-linear digital editing system and utilizing Adobe After Effects software on a Power Macintosh computer.
