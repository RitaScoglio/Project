Ek Chotisi Love Story  ( . The film is an adaptation of Krzysztof Kieślowskis A Short Film About Love (Krótki film o miłości 1988). Released amidst a lot of controversy the movie generated tremendous response at the box office becoming one of the few "Hit" of the year. er

==Plot==
It is a story of 15-year-old Aditya (Aditya Seal) and his voyeuristic love of his nameless neighbor (Manisha Koirala) featuring bathroom masturbation scenes &amp;mdash; which are virtually unseen in Bollywood. Soon Aditya’s hobby turns into infatuation as he begins to follow her movements closely. He feels sexually drawn towards her when he sees her coming out of her bath, her hair dripping wet. He feels jealous when he catches her making love with her boyfriend (Ranvir Shorey).

Manisha is unaware of her secret admirer. When the boy musters up the courage and speaks his heart to her, she takes it in her stride and gives him a little teaser on adolescent love.

==Controversy==
The film created some controversy when Koirala asked for a court order to stay the release of the film pending deletion of some shots she found to be objectionable.She claimed that she was impersonated by someone else in some shots.
