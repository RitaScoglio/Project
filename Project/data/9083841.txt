==Reception==
The film recorded a loss of $101,334.  It caused tension between Raft and Warner Bros, to whom he was under long term contract, because in this United Artists film, Raft played a gangster who loses in the end - the sort of role he had refused to play for Warner Bros. Everett Aaker, The Films of George Raft, McFarland & Company, 2013 p 91
