 1967 film Italian film director Luchino Visconti, based on Albert Camus novel The Stranger (novel)|LÉtranger, with Marcello Mastroianni.   

==Plot==
 
Mersaults friend Sintès beats his girlfriend and is sued by her. At court Mersault testifies to his friends advantage. Sintès is off the hook but now the girls male relatives stalk Mersault. He shoots one of them and ends up in prison.
