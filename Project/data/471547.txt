Boogie Boy is a 1998 film featuring Traci Lords as Shonda.  It was written and directed by Craig Hamann and produced by Braddon Mendelson.  It also stars Mark Dacascos, Emily Lloyd, Jaimz Woolvett, Frederic Forrest, Joan Jett and Linnea Quigley (cameo). Director Craig Hamann is known for co-starring in Quentin Tarantinos unfinished film My Best Friends Birthday.
