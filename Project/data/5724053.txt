  1914 United American comedy silent film made at the Keystone Studios and starring Charlie Chaplin.

== Plot == Roscoe "Fatty" Arbuckle.

==Cast==
* Charles Chaplin - Lord Helpus/Mr. Dovey
* Edgar Kennedy - Lord Helpus Butler
* Minta Durfee - The Lady
* Eva Nelson - Maid

== Rediscovery ==
Cruel, Cruel Love was presumed a lost film for 50 years until a reasonable condition, complete nitrate copy was discovered in South America. Copies were made and its original, two reel format is available for sale.
