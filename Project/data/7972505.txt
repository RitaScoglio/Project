Straight Out of Compton (1999 film)
 
 
 
Straight Out of Compton, released in 1999, is the first movie directed by Ryan Combs.
 Compton local, racist politician laundering money. He manages this through the help of his friends Hump, Johnnie and McClure. The film is loosely based on the life and times of The Majillionaire and tells the story of four gangstars just trying to do their thing and get straight outta compton.
