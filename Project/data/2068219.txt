White Skin ( .  It was released on video in the United States under the title Cannibal.  This film won the CityTV award for Best Canadian First Feature Film at the 2004 Toronto International Film Festival. In this movie, two men discover that ones girlfriend isnt human at all... shes a vampiric creature that needs to feed on human flesh.

Tagline: Dont run...She will find you.
