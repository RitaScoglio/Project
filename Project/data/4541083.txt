==Soundtrack==
Ghoongats soundtrack composed by Amjad Bobby was a raging success even before the films release. Some famous tracks include "Dekha Jo Chehra Tera", "Kaisey Jeeyey Merey Bin", "Yeh Mausam Yeh  Tanhai" and "Dholak Ki Thaap Pey".
