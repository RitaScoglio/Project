The Urethra Chronicles is a 1999 documentary film starring the American pop punk band Blink-182. The film, directed by former manager Rick DeVoe, is a behind the scenes look at the bands history featuring their usual toilet humor. The documentary includes exclusive live performance footage and music videos.
