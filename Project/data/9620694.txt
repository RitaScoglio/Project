 Italian docudrama, Nicola Sacco and Bartolomeo Vanzetti.

The films soundtrack was composed and conducted by Ennio Morricone.

The song "Heres to You (song)|Heres to You" introduced in this film (sung by Joan Baez), was also included in several later films.
