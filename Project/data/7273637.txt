 1944 film starring Nina Foch, based on a story by Griffin Jay and directed by Henry Levin.

==Plot summary== Romany princess wolf at will, just like her late mother. When she learns that Marie LaTours tomb has been discovered, she decides to use her talent to kill everyone who knows the location, because it is a sacred secret that only her people are allowed to know.
