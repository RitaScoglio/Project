  1912 United American silent silent short short comedy film starring William Garwood and Florence La Badie.

==Plot==
Only lasting 15 minutes, it is a light-hearted comedy about the battle between the sexes as several married couples go on a campsite|camp-out together. The women soon realize that the men expect them to do perform all of the work while they relax, leading to several comedic situations.
