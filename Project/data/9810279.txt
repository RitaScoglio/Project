 1982 film set in the world of competitive tennis.    It was produced with the participation at the Canadian Film Development Corporation and Famous Players Limited.

==Plot==
Two girls competing in a tennis match suddenly decide to spend 24 hours doing every crazy thing they ever dreamed of. The girls crazy escapade ends up producing unexpected results.
