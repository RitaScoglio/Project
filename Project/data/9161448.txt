The Mighty Barnum is a 1934 film starring Wallace Beery as P.T. Barnum.  The movie was written by Gene Fowler and Bess Meredyth, and directed by Walter Lang.  Beery had played Barnum four years earlier in A Ladys Morals, a highly fictionalized biography of singer Jenny Lind.  The supporting cast features Adolphe Menjou, Virginia Bruce as Jenny Lind, and Rochelle Hudson.
