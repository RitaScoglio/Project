==Outline==
The film is based on Secrets (play)|Secrets by Michael Palin and Terry Jones  which was broadcast by the BBC as a television play in 1973. It is about a chocolate factory that begins producing popular chocolates which contain human flesh.

The Time Out Film Guide describes the recipe for this film and concludes that of the result: "the consistency should be lumpy and the taste insipid."
