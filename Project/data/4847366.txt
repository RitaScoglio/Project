Crossing the Bridge: The Sound of Istanbul

}} 2005 film/Documentary documentary directed by Fatih Akın. The film is a journey through the music scene in modern Istanbul, Turkey as well as portraying its cultural life. It was screened out of competition at the 2005 Cannes Film Festival.
