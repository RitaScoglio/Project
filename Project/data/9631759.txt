==Plot==
John Sheldon is falsely accused by Frank Nolan of killing Lou Salters.  With his owner now in prison, Nolan steals Sheldons horse Rex with plans to ride him in a big race.  Sheldons dog, Rinty, Alice Ingram and her sidekick Henry rescue Rex and Alice rides him in the race instead.  The winnings from the race are used to pay for Sheldons defense.
