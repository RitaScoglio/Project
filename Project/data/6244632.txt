==Reception==
As of September 2014, the movie has a 2% score on ratings aggregator Rotten Tomatoes based on sixty-two reviews. 

Crossover also did poorly at the box office, earning roughly U.S. Dollar|US$3.7 million on opening weekend. However, thanks to an exceptionally small budget, the film has managed to garner a relatively fair amount of success, grossing just over $7 million by the end of its short-lived 29 days in theaters and only costing $5.8 million to produce.
