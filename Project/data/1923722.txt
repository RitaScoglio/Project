==Plot==
It tells a series of stories about love affairs or illicit meetings involving a prostitute, a soldier, a chambermaid, her employers son, a married woman, her husband, a young girl, a poet, an actress and a count. At the end of each encounter, one of the partners forms a liaison with another person, and so on.
