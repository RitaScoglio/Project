==Plot==

Drama about a 12-year-old boy who fantasises about having enough money to be able to cure his grandmothers serious heart condition. When he finds himself in a haunted house, the mysterious owner grants him one wish - the Midas touch. The boy soon learns that it is more of a curse than a blessing when everything he touches turns into gold.

==Genre(s)==

Drama, Comedy, Fantasy, Independent Film
