== Plot ==

Khaled falls in love with his friend Suhair. His father abandoned him and his family for another woman. He struggles to overcome the challenges and finally graduates from Law school. He strives to fight for the right of the workers in Suhairs fathers firm. A bitter relationship results between the father and Khalid, but Khalid wins the case. He marries Suhair, who had supported him all along.
