The Wild, Wild Rose
 
  Hong Kong film directed by Wang Tian-lin.  The story line and the songs melodies are derived from the opera Carmen.

==Cast and roles==
* Yeung Sha Fei Au - Hanhuas Mother
* Grace Chang - Deng Sijia, nicknamed "The Wild, Wild Rose"
* Lei Da - Old Wang
* Liu Enjia - Fatty Lin
* Ma Li - Old Tian
* Ma Xiaonong - Old Wangs Wife
* Shen Yun - Li Meimei
* Su Feng - Wu Suxin
* Tang Di - Sijias Husband
* Tien Ching - Xiao Liu
* Wang Lai - Xueli Zhang Yang - Liang Hanhua
