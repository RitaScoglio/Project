 2007 Sweden|Swedish drama film directed by Johan Kling, about an irresponsible young woman in central Stockholm who befriends an older man. The film was generally well received by critics. 

==Cast==
* Michelle Meadows as Eva
* Michael Segerström as Bernard
* Richard Ulfsäter as Micke
* Michael Lindgren as Nico
* Marko Ivkovich as McDonalds chef
* Erica Silfverhielm as Martina
* Henrik Lundström as Mickes friend
* Tanja Lorentzon as Maja
