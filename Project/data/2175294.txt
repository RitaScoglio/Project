 family drama Walt Disney Productions. It is based on a novel of the same name by Ian Niall.

Vera Miles and Brian Keith star. This was Indian actor Sabu Dastagir|Sabus last film, released only a few months after his death.

==Plot==
A mistreated Bengal tiger named Raja escapes from a traveling circus, and hides in the woods surrounding the small town of Scotia. The new arrival starts a panic, and the townsfolk want Raja killed with the exception of Julie Williams (played by Pamela Franklin), the sheriffs daughter.
 National Guard, who are under orders to shoot the tiger on sight.
