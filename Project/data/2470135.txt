==Reception==
In the Army Now received almost universally negative reviews, holding a 6% on Rotten Tomatoes based on 31 reviews.  The consensus states "This 1994 Pauly Shore vehicle stretches its stars thin shtick to the breaking point with a laugh-deficient screenplay that borrows shamelessly from Bill Murrays far superior Stripes (film)|Stripes".
