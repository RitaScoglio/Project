Lyckantropen
Lyckantropen Swedish short film written and directed by Steve Ericsson. It follows a family in a small town that struggles with relationship balance and internal emotional conflicts. The film is only 28 minutes long, and features almost no dialogue throughout.
 Norwegian avant-garde music|avant-garde electronica / black metal band Ulver.
