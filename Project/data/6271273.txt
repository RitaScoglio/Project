}} Gothic short story The Fall of the House of Usher by Edgar Allan Poe. Future director Luis Buñuel co-wrote the screenplay with Epstein, his second film credit, having previously worked as assistant director on Epsteins film Mauprat#Film adaptation|Mauprat from 1926.

American critic Roger Ebert included the film on his list of "Great Movies." 

In 1995, Ivan Fedele composed an original soundtrack for the film.
