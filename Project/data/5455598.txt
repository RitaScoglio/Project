 John Mack Brown, Jean Harlow, Clark Gable, Marjorie Rambeau and Ralph Bellamy.  The film was written by Frances Marion and directed by George W. Hill for MGM.

==Box office==
According to MGM records the film earned $708,000 in the US and Canada and $286,000 elsewhere resulting in a profit of $148,000.
