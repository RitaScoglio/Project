The restored version premiered at the Walter Reade Theater in New York City on 29 September 2012, as part of the 2012 New York Film Festival, and was released on DVD and Blu-ray in November 2012.  On 25 November 2012, it was shown by BBC Two as part of BBCs "The Rolling Stones at 50" celebrations. According to BBCs website, it was the first TV broadcast of the 47 year old documentary.
