==Plot==
Luke Wilson appears as good-hearted ex-con Wendell Baker working in a retirement home, with Lukes other brother, Owen Wilson, as the homes head nurse. Eva Mendes co-stars as Luke Wilsons characters love-interest, Doreen.  Baker, along with three residents of the home, help him win Doreen back; and, Baker helps them fight the corrupt staff. The film also features Eddie Griffin, Kris Kristofferson, Harry Dean Stanton, Seymour Cassel and Will Ferrell.
