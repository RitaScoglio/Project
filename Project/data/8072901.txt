The film is about the marriage of a gynecologist named David and his wife Marianne. The films score was composed by Dag Wirén.

==Reception==
A Lesson in Love received generally positive reviews from film critics. Review aggregator Rotten Tomatoes reports 100% approval (based on five critics), with an average rating of 7.2/10.
