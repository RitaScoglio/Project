  1971  Argentine musical musical comedy film directed and written by Enrique Carreras with Norberto Aroldi.    The film premiered on 30 July 1971 in Buenos Aires and stars Palito Ortega and Mercedes Carreras. The movie was filmed in Mar del Plata, Buenos Aires.
