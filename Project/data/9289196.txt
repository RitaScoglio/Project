 1991  Malayalam  drama film Murali in Carnatic classical and semi classical. The songs were composed by Raveendran.
 National Film Best Actor Special Jury Best Male Singer (K. J. Yesudas) for the song "Rama Katha Ganalayam". On the centenary of Indian cinema in April 2013, Forbes (India)|Forbes included Mohanlals performance in the film on its list, "25 Greatest Acting Performances of Indian Cinema". 

== Plot == Lakshmi and trauma reaches penultimate when his family comes to know about Ramans death and that Gopi was concealing it. Everything ends fine when the family understands his intentions.
