 Walt Disney Productions starring James Garner and Vera Miles. The supporting cast includes Pat Hingle, Jay Silverheels, and an 11-year-old Jodie Foster, and the plotline involves a cavalry soldiers misadventures with a camel and a little boy. The film was written by Harry Spalding and directed by Bernard McEveety.

The movie earned an estimated $2 million in North American rentals.
