Døden på Oslo S is a Norwegian film, released in 1990 in film|1990. The title literally meaning Death at Oslo Central Station, it is set in Oslo in the late eighties. It is based on a book by Ingvar Ambjørnsen from the book series Pelle og Proffen.

==Plot==
The film is about the two teenage boys, Pelle and Proffen, that try to help Pelles girlfriend, Lena, who has a drug problem. She is only 15 years old, and they initially meet at a snackbar in downtown Oslo. She has been abused by a social worker at a home for troubled teens. While they try to help Lena she runs away, they get beat up and watch people overdose at the central train station in Oslo.

The film deals with issues like drugs, troubled teens and their parents, child pornography. Its also about Pelle and Proffens families and their different backgrounds. Pelles parents are former hippies and very liberal, but Proffens parents are old and conservative.
