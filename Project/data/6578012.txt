 1992 neo-noir black comedy written and directed by Alien Castle and produced by Donald P. Borchers that film stars Sherilyn Fenn, Whip Hubley, David Hewlett, David Johansen and Paul Bartel.   

==Plot==
A husband and wife check into the Sunset Motel in Anaheim, California in 1955 where affairs and other sexual crimes soon erupt.
