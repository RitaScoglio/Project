No Skin Off My Ass is a 1993 comedy film|comedy-drama film by Bruce LaBruce.
 punk hairdresser Fifth Column band members Caroline Azar and Beverly Breckenridge.

No Skin Off My Ass provides a template for many of the themes in LaBruces later movies. Explicit sex scenes between LaBruces character and von Bruckers are interwoven with a radical political message espoused by Jones, whose character directs a film called "Girls of the Symbionese Liberation Army|SLA".

No Skin Off My Ass played film festivals around the world and quickly became a cult film. Famously, Kurt Cobain declared it his favourite film.
