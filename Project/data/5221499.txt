Chilbeontong sosageon (Incident of the 7th Bamboo Flute) is a 1936 Korean film directed by and starring Na Woon-gyu. It premiered at the WooMiKwan theater.

==Plot==
This action-oriented film tells the story of a traveling theatrical troupe. When one of the actresses is sold to criminals running an opium den, Na Woon-gyus character rescues her and returns her to the troupe.
