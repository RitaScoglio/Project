Wuroemae from the Outside, Part II (외계에서 온 우뢰매 2) (Thunder Hawk from Beyond, Part 2) was the second in the Ureme series of Korean childrens science-fiction films directed by Kim Cheong-gi and starring Shim Hyung-rae.
