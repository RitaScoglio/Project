Wax or the Discovery of Television Among the Bees is the first independent feature film to have been edited on a digital Non-linear editing system|non-linear system. It is also the first film (independent or otherwise) to have been re-formatted as hypertext and posted on the internet. The New York Times recognized the accomplishment, and ran the article "Cult Film is First on the Internet" in its May 23, 1993 business section.
