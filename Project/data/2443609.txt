}} 2004 Cinema Spanish film directed by José Luis Garci and starring Elsa Pataky, María Adánez and Carlos Hipólito.

The film was nominated for six Goya Awards in 2005, and won the award for Best Production Design.

==Plot== Second World War, the film offers a nostalgic vision of a city managing to sustain entertainment, hope and love in the face of post-war hardship.
