 1930 MGM early sound drama/film, produced and directed by Sam Wood.

==Synopsis== German immigrant barber on the verge of becoming rich, takes his sickly son away on a two-year stay in a drier climate. The mans partner deems him a failure, but he learns that his family is more important than finance.
