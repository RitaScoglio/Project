Operation "Happy New Year!" ( , Operatsiya „S novym godom!“) is a 1996 Russian comedy film, a follow-up to the Peculiarities of National Hunt,  directed by Aleksandr Rogozhkin.

Starring the film are Aleksej Buldakov, Sergej Makovetskij,  Leonid Jarmolnik, and Viktor Bychkov.

==Synopsis==
The hunters meet in an emergency room on a   yin and yang style, or why vodka without beer is a waste of money.
