 1967 Cinema Indian Kannada Kannada film, directed by C. V. Raju and produced by Smt Jayalakshmi. The film stars K. S. Ashwath, R. Nagendra Rao, Udaykumar and Rajashankar in lead roles. The film had musical score by C. N. Panduranga.
