==Plot summary==
 
In 1941, Colonel Lawton (Lewis Stone) of the 19th Cavalry Regiment has to convert his unit from horses to tanks. Sergeant Patrick Aloysius Hap Doan (Wallace Beery) has a hard time with the adjustments.
