== Synopsis ==
Two teens, Birdy (Modine) and Al (Cage), become friends at school and serve in Vietnam War|Vietnam. Birdy has long been disturbingly fixated with birds and his Vietnam experiences push him over the edge: returning from the war, he is hospitalized for a psychiatric assessment and Al tries to reach him before hell be separated from Birdy, leaving him alone and lost. As the story develops, flashback scenes show their lives as teens in a working-class neighborhood of Philadelphia in the United States in the 1960s, and their developing friendship and viewpoints.

== Reception == Grand Prize of the Jury at the 1985 Cannes Film Festival.     Roger Ebert gave the film four stars (out of four),  and at Rotten Tomatoes currently (2011) it has a rating of 88% based on 25 reviews. Halliwells Film Guide claimed the film "somewhat lacks plot advancement" and is "too self-indulgent even to earn marks for trying."
