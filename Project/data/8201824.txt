Tiger Shark is a 1932 film directed by Howard Hawks and starring Edward G. Robinson, Richard Arlen and Zita Johann. The movie was made the same year as Scarface (1932 film)|Scarface, which is widely acknowledged to be the directors best film of the early sound era. The general storyline was repeated several times in subsequent films, most notably Manpower (1941 film)|Manpower with Marlene Dietrich and George Raft, in which Robinson plays the same role, only as a power line worker.
 The Mummy that same year.

==Plot==
The plot concerns a one-handed tuna fisherman named Mike (Robinson) whose wife falls for the man he lost his hand saving.
