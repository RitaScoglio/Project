La cabalgata del circo (  musical film directed and written by Mario Soffici with Eduardo Boneo and Francisco Madrid. It stars Libertad Lamarque and Hugo del Carril.

==Plot Outline==
The lives, loves and vicissitudes of a group of circus performers in the Argentine Pampas around the turn of the century are played out in this drama with songs.
