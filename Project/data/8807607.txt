}} Argentine horror film directed and written by Gustavo Cova and Horacio Maldonado. The film starred Horacio Erman. The horror film was released in English speaking countries as Somebody is Hanging Around.

==Synopsis==
A group of students are put under by North American scientists to experience the effects of a new drug that allow to share dreams. The consequences are disastrous.
