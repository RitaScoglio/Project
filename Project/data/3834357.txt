Live at Budokan is a DVD by pop rock Singer-songwriter|singer/songwriter Butch Walker, released on December 13, 2005 by Epic Records. It contains two concerts &mdash; a 40-minute full-band set, recorded in Tokyo, Japan during Walkers 2005 tour with Avril Lavigne, and 75-minute acoustic set, recorded in Atlanta, Georgia in 2003.

==Content==

===Live at Budokan=== bootleg clips of two covers played during the tour of Elvis Costellos "Radio Radio" and Blur (band)|Blurs "Song 2".

===This is me... Justified and Stripped===
As a bonus, the DVD also features an acoustic concert filmed between the releases of Left of Self-Centered (2002) and Letters (Butch Walker album)|Letters (2004). The set includes tracks from both albums, as well as many Marvelous 3 tunes and fractions of cover versions in between songs, including the Carpenters "Close to You", Maxi Priests "Close to You", Nellys "Pimp Juice" and  OutKasts "Ms. Jackson". The concert was filmed on June 7, 2003 at the Variety Playhouse in Atlanta, Georgia. Excerpts of it can also be found on Walkers 2004 live album This Is Me... Justified and Stripped.
