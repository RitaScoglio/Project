Gudachari No.1
Gudachari 1983 Telugu Telugu film directed by Kodi Ramakrishna. It stars Chiranjeevi and Radhika Sarathkumar in important roles.

==Plot==

Vijay (Chiranjeevi) is a CBI agent who lives in Delhi. On a special operation, Vijay is sent a place where a gang is involved in a crime. His job is to gain evidence against them and bring them to justice. Vijay succeeds in bursting the gang and in the due process, he meets Rekha (Radhika Sarathkumar) and falls for her. Vijay manages to liquidate the boss of that gang and returns to Delhi with Rekha as his wife.
